|CelMod| Props

Spawnable Props for use with !spawn
Usage: !spawn <name of prop> <options>
Ex. !spawn woodcrate1 drop
OR
Ex. !woodcrate1 drop

Options include:
unfrozen - Spawns the prop unfrozen
nogod - Spawns the prop and makes it breakable
drop - Spawns the prop at the floor
